Welcome to Entri Academia

Entri is an e-learning app that helps people learn job skills in local languages to improve their employability. The app offers over 500 courses, including videos, mock exams, study cards, and podcasts.Entri-app is a Kochi-brewed vernacular learning platform that aims at helping people land their dream job by empowering them with the right skill set & expert guidance at an affordable cost. Founded in 2017, Entri.app is now one of the most sought-after vernacularlearning apps in the country offering upskilling & test preparation courses.
